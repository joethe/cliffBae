This is an awful game that we are making!



This is a game that we created at CarlHacks 2016, a Hackathon hosted by the Carleton college. Teams are given 36 hours to create a project of their choice, and make it happen.

Our project is to recreate a board game called Fjords which some of the members of our group are a great fan of, but is now (sadly) out of print. Fjords is a 2-player strategy game where players place terrain tiles and create a unique board, before creating farms to claim their territory and fend off the enemy farmer.
